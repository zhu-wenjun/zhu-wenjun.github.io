经典台词

《功夫熊猫》

You can't leave, real warrior never quits. 你不能走，真正的勇士从不退出。

One always meets its destiny on the road he takes to avoid. 人往往在逃避的道路上遇到自己的命运。

There are no accidents. 没有巧合。

If you only do what you can do, you will never be more than you are now. 如果你只做你能做的事儿，你永远不能超越现在的自己。

Yesterday is history, tomrrow is a mystery. 昨日已成历史，明日不期而遇。

Today is a gift, that why it's called the present. 今天是礼物，所以我们称今天为礼物。
